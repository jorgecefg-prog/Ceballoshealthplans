Who We Are

Ceballos Health Plans is operated by Jorge Ceballos, a licensed insurance agent. We help individuals, families, and groups find personalized health insurance coverage through our intake form at ceballoshealthplans.com.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your rights regarding your personal data.

2

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when completing our health insurance intake form, including:

  • Full legal name, date of birth, gender, and Social Security Number (SSN)
  • Contact information — email address, phone number, and home address
  • Health and medical history, current medications, and pre-existing conditions
  • Household members, spouse/partner, and dependent information
  • Financial information — income range and payment details (debit/credit or checking account)
  • Beneficiary information
  • Doctor, pharmacy, and primary care physician information
  • Electronic signature and authorization
3

How We Use Your Information

  • To prepare and present personalized health insurance quotes for you
  • To contact you by phone, text, or email about your health insurance inquiry
  • To submit health insurance applications to carriers on your behalf with your consent
  • To process premium payments securely
  • To comply with applicable insurance regulations and legal requirements
  • To follow up on your interest in related products such as life insurance, annuities, or will and trust services — if you selected any of those options
4

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties — ever.

We may share your information only with:

  • Insurance carriers — to obtain quotes or process applications you authorize
  • Formspree — our secure form submission processor, bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Netlify — our website hosting provider, which does not access your form data
  • Legal authorities — when required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights
5

TCPA & Communications Consent

By submitting our form and checking the TCPA consent box, you expressly authorize Ceballos Health Plans and its licensed representatives to contact you at the phone number provided using:

  • Automated dialing systems (ATDS)
  • Prerecorded or artificial voice messages
  • AI-generated voice technology
  • SMS or MMS text messages

Consent is not required as a condition of purchasing insurance. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message.

6

Payment & Financial Data Security

Payment information you provide — including debit/credit card numbers and checking account details — is transmitted using SSL encryption (HTTPS) and stored securely by our form processor. We do not store full card numbers or bank account numbers on our own servers.

Social Security Numbers entered on the form are masked visually for your protection and transmitted only over encrypted connections.

7

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal and regulatory obligations, and resolve any disputes. TCPA consent records are retained for a minimum of 5 years as required by law.

9

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use minimal session data to maintain form state. We do not use advertising tracking pixels, retargeting cookies, or third-party analytics. The UserWay accessibility widget on our site may collect anonymized usage data for accessibility improvement purposes.

10

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. Dependent information collected as part of a family plan application is provided by the adult applicant and used solely for insurance purposes.

11

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our form after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
